# Terms of Service — Humboldt Laundry

**DRAFT — Review with a California attorney before launch.**

Last updated: June 2025

## 1. Service description

Humboldt Laundry ("we," "us") connects customers with independent contractor drivers who pick up, wash, fold, and return laundry in Humboldt County, California.

## 2. Customer responsibilities

- Provide accurate pickup address and access instructions
- Disclose detergent sensitivities, delicates, and stain treatment preferences
- Be available during scheduled pickup windows or authorize contactless pickup
- Pay agreed fees upon order completion

## 3. Limitation of liability

- We are a marketplace platform, not a dry cleaner
- Drivers are independent contractors, not employees
- Maximum liability per order: lesser of order total or $100
- We are not liable for pre-existing garment damage, shrinkage of improperly labeled items, or allergic reactions when customer does not disclose sensitivities

## 4. Lost or damaged items

- Report within 24 hours of delivery
- Maximum reimbursement: $50 per garment, $200 per order unless itemized receipt provided
- Items not claimed within 7 days of ready status are donated or disposed

## 5. Cancellations

- Customer may cancel free before driver accepts
- After acceptance: $5 cancellation fee
- No-show at pickup window: full pickup fee charged

## 6. Payments

- Prices displayed at booking; final charge may adjust for extra bags with driver confirmation
- Payment processed via Stripe; receipts emailed

## 7. Disputes

- Contact care@humboldtlaundry.com within 48 hours
- Governed by California law; Humboldt County venue

## 8. Privacy

- We collect name, address, phone, email, and order history
- Driver location shared only during active deliveries
- ID documents stored securely; not shared with customers
