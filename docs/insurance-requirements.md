# Insurance & Safety Requirements — Drivers

**DRAFT — Review with a California attorney and insurance broker before launch.**

## Required before first paid job

| Requirement | Minimum | Verification |
|-------------|---------|--------------|
| Valid government ID | — | Upload during onboarding |
| Driver's license | Current CA license | If using vehicle for pickup |
| Auto liability insurance | CA state minimum ($15k/$30k/$5k) | Self-attestation MVP; proof upload Phase 2 |
| Renters/home liability | $100k recommended | Self-attestation MVP |

## Strongly recommended

- **Garment care liability**: Discuss with broker whether homeowners/renters policy covers damage to customer property in your care
- **Commercial auto**: If driving primarily for laundry pickups
- **Umbrella policy**: $1M for additional protection

## Safety checklist (every job)

- [ ] Bags sealed and labeled with customer name
- [ ] No mixing loads
- [ ] Customer notes reviewed (delicates, stains, detergent)
- [ ] Vehicle clean and dry for transport
- [ ] Proof-of-pickup photo if customer not home (with prior consent)
- [ ] Cell phone charged (rural Humboldt coverage gaps)

## Incident reporting

Report within 24 hours via admin or driver app:
- Lost or damaged garment
- Customer dispute
- Vehicle accident during job
- Allergic reaction claim

## Background checks

- Consent collected at onboarding
- Run via Checkr or similar before 10th completed job (Phase 2)
- Disqualifiers: violent felonies, theft convictions in past 7 years

## Humboldt-specific notes

- Mud season (Nov–Mar): allow extra travel time; communicate delays via app
- Long rural driveways: confirm address; don't enter private property without permission
- Limited cell service: download job details before leaving service area
